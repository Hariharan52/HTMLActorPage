Vijay

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, popularly known as Vijay or "Thalapathy", is a renowned Indian actor primarily working in Tamil cinema. Having appeared in 68 films throughout his career, he's recognized as one of the most commercially successful and highly paid actors in India. After a three-decade-long career in the film industry, Vijay has announced his transition into politics, launching his party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK), in February 2024. His final film, "Jana Nayagan", a mega-budget political action thriller, is slated for release on January 9, 2026, marking his farewell to the silver screen before he focuses fully on his political aspirations. He has recently addressed political rallies and outlined his party's stance, opposing divisive politics and criticizing both the BJP and the ruling DMK.
